Pasta

Description

Pasta is most commonly an italian dish. It can be prepared in many different ways.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Parmesan
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Place Pasta in boiling water
  3. Let it cook for 30 minutes
  4. Drain the water
  5. Apply tomato sauce as desired
  6. Top with parmesan cheese
