Pizza

Description

Pizza is everyone's favorite food. It is effortlesly simple to make.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Mix water and yeast, then let it rest.
  2. Add yeast mix to flour, let dough rise.
  3. Knead and roll into flat circle.
  4. Apply tomato sauce as desired.
  5. Top with toppings of choice.
  6. Place in oven for 20 minutes.
